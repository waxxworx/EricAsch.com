The Practice

Ways of walking
the path together

All of these offerings grow from the same root — an invitation to meet yourself fully, with honesty and without turning away.

Offering

Mindfulness Meditation

Learning to be present with what is — gently, persistently, without judgment. Whether you are brand new to meditation or returning to a lapsed practice, these sessions offer a grounded, accessible entry point into the transformative power of simply paying attention.

Taught at Sacred Roots in Charlotte, NC, and online via Zoom. Offered on a love-offering basis.

In person & online  ·  Love-offering basis

Offering

Men's Work

Spaces for men to gather and see and be seen by other men. Exploring our deep potential to live in integrity — to be strong and also vulnerable. This is not therapy, and it is not performance. It is men sitting together in honest inquiry about what it means to be alive and fully present in our lives.

In person  ·  Charlotte, NC

Offering

Rites of Passage

Marking thresholds with intention. Crossing from one chapter of life into the next. These are ceremonial experiences designed to honor the transitions that matter — from boyhood to manhood, from one life to another, from loss toward renewal. Every threshold deserves to be crossed with witnesses.

In person  ·  By arrangement
